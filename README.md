# ⚡️ ElectroFix

> Multi-page website for electrical installation and maintenance services.

---

## ✨ About

ElectroFix is a modern multi-page website created for a company specializing in electrical installation works for residential, commercial, and industrial facilities.

The platform provides detailed information about services, pricing, company projects, and contact options for clients.

---

## 🔌 Services

- ⚡️ Electrical installation
- 🏠 Wiring for apartments and houses
- 🏢 Commercial electrical systems
- 💡 Lighting installation
- 🔧 Electrical repair & maintenance
- 🛡️ Safety inspections and diagnostics

---

- 🌐 Multi-page navigation
